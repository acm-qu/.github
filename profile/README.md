## 🌟 Our Vision
ACM-QU aims to be a leading force in fostering **innovation**, **collaboration**, and **excellence** in computing and technology among QU students. We bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world application for whoever interested in the Tech field.

### 🎯 Key Objectives
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Promoting learning through student-led workshops and tech talks.
- **Innovation:** Encouraging research in areas like AI, Deep Learning, and Agentic Systems.
- **Community:** Building a vibrant network of tech enthusiasts and professionals.
